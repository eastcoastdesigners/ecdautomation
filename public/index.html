08 — Refunds and Cancellations

Our refund policy.

We want every client to be happy with what we build. Our refund policy is straightforward.

01 — Before project starts

If you've paid your deposit but we haven't started building, you can request a full refund within 7 days. After 7 days, your deposit is non-refundable because we've blocked a build slot for you.

02 — During the build

If you cancel mid-project, you receive a partial refund based on what work hasn't been started. Specific amounts are detailed in your project agreement.

03 — After launch

Once the project is delivered and you've received the code, the project fee is non-refundable. Care Package subscriptions can be cancelled any time with 30 days notice.

04 — Disputes

If something goes wrong, contact us first. We respond within 24 hours and work hard to make it right. We've never had a chargeback and we'd like to keep it that way.
